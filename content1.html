No-Bake Peanut Butter Pie

Ingredients

IngredientAmount
Graham cracker crust1 (9-inch)
Creamy peanut butter1 cup
Cream cheese (softened)8 oz
Powdered sugar1 cup
Cool Whip (thawed)8 oz

Instructions

  1. Beat peanut butter and cream cheese until smooth.
  2. Add powdered sugar and mix well.
  3. Fold in Cool Whip until fluffy.
  4. Spoon into crust and smooth top.
  5. Chill 2 to 3 hours or overnight.
  6. Garnish with toppings if desired.
