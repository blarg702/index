No-Bake Pink Lemonade Pie

Ingredients

IngredientAmount
Golden Oreos20
Salted butter (melted)4 tbsp
Milk1 cup
Instant vanilla pudding mix1 (3.4 oz) box
Pink lemonade concentrate (thawed)6 oz
Cool Whip (thawed)8 oz

Instructions

  1. Crush Oreos and mix with melted butter; press into pie pan.
  2. Whisk milk and pudding mix until thickened.
  3. Stir in pink lemonade concentrate.
  4. Fold in Cool Whip until smooth.
  5. Pour mixture into crust and smooth top.
  6. Freeze 4 hours or until firm.
