No-Bake Mint Chocolate Cookie Ice Cream Pie

Ingredients

IngredientAmount
Mint Chocolate Chip Ice Cream3 pints
Chocolate cookie pie crust1
Crushed mint chocolate Oreos1 1/2 cups
Whipped CreamFor topping

Instructions

  1. Scoop 1 1/2 pints ice cream into crust and smooth.
  2. Sprinkle 1 cup crushed cookies, press lightly.
  3. Freeze 1 hour until solid.
  4. Top with remaining 1 1/2 pints ice cream.
  5. Top with whipped cream and 1/2 cup Oreos.
  6. Freeze at least 2 hours.
